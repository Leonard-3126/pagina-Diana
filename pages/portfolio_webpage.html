About Me

A little introduction

Hey there! I'm Diana, a Peruvian girl living in Italy, always on the move and never able to stay put for long.

I’m a huge fan of Spongebob (and yes, I also dream of getting my driver's license just like him!).

I once thought I'd be a psychologist or a lawyer, but reading was never quite my thing.

So, by pure chance, I stumbled into the world of art direction and honestly, it’s been the best “mistake” I could have ever made.

Now, I’m constantly inspired, eager to learn, and excited to see where this creative journey will take me.
